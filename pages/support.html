Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start my day in SOTO?

1. Open the SOTO app
2. Enter your PIN or use Face ID
3. Tap "Start Day"
4. Confirm your location
5. Begin your route

How do I submit expenses?

1. Tap "Expenses" in the main menu
2. Select "Add Expense"
3. Take a photo of your receipt
4. Enter amount and category
5. Tap "Submit"

How do I reset my PIN?

1. Go to Settings
2. Tap "Security"
3. Select "Change PIN"
4. Enter your current PIN
5. Set a new 4-digit PIN

Why isn't my location updating?

1. Check that Location Services is enabled
2. Ensure SOTO has location permissions
3. Verify your internet connection
4. Try restarting the app

How do I access the admin panel?

Admin access is granted by your organization. Contact your administrator to request admin privileges or reset your access.
