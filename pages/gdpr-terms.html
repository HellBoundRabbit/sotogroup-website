Terms & Privacy Policy (GDPR)

This document explains how SOTO Routes collects, uses, and protects your personal data in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

1. Data we collect

We collect and process the following personal data to operate the SOTO Routes driver portal:

  • Name, email address, and contact details
  • Home postcode (for route optimisation and logistics)
  • Profile photo (display picture)
  • Work availability (working days, standby days)
  • Location data (only when you start/end your day or record collections/deliveries, for compliance and logistics)
  • Expense claims and related documents (receipts, mileage, etc.)

2. How we use your data

  • To manage driver schedules and route assignments
  • To process expense claims and reimbursements
  • To communicate with you about work-related matters
  • To comply with legal and operational requirements

3. Legal basis

We process your data based on your consent (when you agree to these terms), the performance of a contract (your employment/engagement with SOTO), and our legitimate interests in operating the service.

4. Data sharing

Your data may be shared with your employer/office and trusted service providers (e.g. cloud hosting) necessary to operate the platform. We do not sell your personal data.

5. Your rights

You have the right to access, rectify, or erase your personal data, restrict processing, data portability, and to withdraw consent. Contact your office administrator or the data controller for requests.

6. Data retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active and as required for legal, accounting, or operational purposes.
