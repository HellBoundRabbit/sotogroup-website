Step 1: Save Your SG Logo

Save your SG logo image as soto-favicon.png in the /assets/logos/ folder.

Step 2: Convert to Favicon Format

Use one of these online tools to convert your PNG to favicon format:

  • Favicon.io Converter
  • Real Favicon Generator
  • Favicon Generator

Step 3: Download Favicon Files

Download these files and place them in /assets/logos/:

  • favicon.ico (main favicon)
  • favicon-16x16.png (16x16 pixels)
  • favicon-32x32.png (32x32 pixels)
  • apple-touch-icon.png (180x180 pixels for iOS)

Step 4: Test Your Favicon

Once you've added the favicon files:

  1. Hard refresh your browser (CMD+SHIFT+R)
  2. Check the browser tab - you should see your SG logo
  3. Check bookmarks - favicon should appear there too

Current Favicon Links (Already Added)

All your HTML pages now have these favicon links:

<!-- Favicon -->
<link rel="icon" type="image/x-icon" href="/assets/logos/favicon.ico">
<link rel="icon" type="image/png" sizes="32x32" href="/assets/logos/favicon-32x32.png">
<link rel="icon" type="image/png" sizes="16x16" href="/assets/logos/favicon-16x16.png">
